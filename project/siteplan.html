Let's Go Dutch Catering

Dutch oven catering company, family owned and operated, specializing in the best food you ever hung a lip on.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide customers with all the information they need about our catering services. Our goal is to showcase our menu, upcoming events, and offer an easy way to book catering for any occasion.

Scenarios

  • What events and fairs is Let's Go Dutch attending?
  • How can I book catering services for my event?

Color Schema

Primary Color:Cowboy Blue (#4162c6) - Used for headings, navigation, and accent buttons.

Accent Color: Saddle Brown (#8B4513) - Used for background, footer and text links.

Typography

Heading Font: "Roboto Slab" for headings (h1, h2, h3).
Body Font: "Lora" for paragraph text and general content.
Accent Font: "Poppins" for buttons and small details like navigation.
