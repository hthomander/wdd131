Our Story

Richard and Cleone Moncur are passionate about bringing people together through the love of tasty food. Together they started 'Let's Go Dutch'! Since then, Let's Go Dutch Catering has expanded throughout the Moncur family where we have made Dutch ovens our specialty. With years of experience and a passion for hearty, home-cooked meals, we bring the warmth of traditional flavors to your special events.
